Maltari,Near Gandhi PG College(Azamgarh)

The library is one heck of a treasure for competitive exams provided you land up in the correct library. If you have access to the Internet along with an access to the relevant books at a good library, you have all the required resources at your disposal. Library is also advantageous in the following ways:

Importance of Libraries

It's peaceful. None of your irritating friends, neighbours, relatives would get a chance to poke their heads into your preparation time, as you would be alone and studying.

Libraries have rules. They don't allow talking over phones so you would be disciplined to not use that.

Seeing fellow library mates studying will motivate and inspire you.

Libraries have timings, so you would start following a strict time table for studying which is always a good thing.

So if you get access to a good library, congratulations you have set yourself on to the path of success.

Uses of Library

They are able to complete their papers and carry out their studies using the material present in a library. Furthermore, libraries are a great place for studying alone or even in groups, without any disturbance.

Moreover, libraries also help in increasing our concentration levels.
